Get Involved / Queer Liberation

Working group

Queer Liberation

LGBTQ+ defense, mutual care, and joy across East Tennessee.

Join this group All working groups

What we do

LGBTQ+ people in East Tennessee face material threats: state legislation targeting trans healthcare, rollbacks of legal protections, and the daily weight of living in a region where visibility can carry real risk. This group organizes in solidarity with queer and trans neighbors, centering defense alongside mutual care and the kind of visible community presence that says we are here and we are not going away.

We are forming now, which means this is the moment to shape the work. If you bring energy, an idea, or simply the conviction that queer liberation belongs on the socialist agenda in Knoxville, you are exactly who we are looking for. Show up and help build this from the start.

What we are working on

This group is forming. The items below are examples of the direction we expect to take, not current commitments. Early members will set the actual priorities.

The three areas pulling us forward:

  • Legislative defense and response. Tracking and responding to state and local attacks on LGBTQ+ rights, especially bills targeting trans youth healthcare, school policies, and public accommodations. Turning out bodies at the statehouse and city hall when it counts.
  • Mutual care and practical support. Connecting queer community members to housing help, legal information, and the chapter’s broader mutual aid network. Neighbors meeting neighbors’ needs directly.
  • Community presence and visibility. Public events, coalition work with existing Knoxville LGBTQ+ organizations, and a clear signal that queer people belong in this organizing space.

What you would actually do

No prior organizing experience required. People joining a forming group usually start with something straightforward and concrete:

  • Come to an early meeting and help decide what this group actually works on.
  • Map the landscape: share what local LGBTQ+ groups, needs, or fights you already know about.
  • Connect us to existing community organizations in Knoxville you have relationships with.
  • Draft a flyer, take meeting notes, or help get the word out to friends who should be here.

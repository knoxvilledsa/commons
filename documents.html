What you see here

Only public-zone materials are posted on this page. Dues-paying members can reach internal records, working drafts, and meeting minutes through Member resources.

Why we publish these

A chapter that asks people to trust it with their time and their dues should be readable from the outside. Posting the documents lets you check the chapter against its own rules before you ever walk into a meeting, and it keeps officers accountable to the same text everyone else can read.

Knoxville Area DSA organizes its rules into a four-tier hierarchy: Bylaws first, then Codes, then Rules, then Policies, with each tier sitting under the one above it. That structure is what keeps the chapter democratic and predictable instead of run by whoever is in the room. You can see how the tiers fit together, and how decisions move through them, on the how the chapter is structured page.

When the membership amends a document or adopts a new one, the version here is updated and re-dated so the public copy always matches what is actually in force.

July General Meeting

Monday · 6:00 PM · Hybrid: online via Zoom plus an in-person spot announced with the agenda

General meetings are how the chapter makes decisions together. Come early, say hello, and see what we are working on. You do not have to be a member to attend, and you can join from your couch.

First time? New folks are greeted and walked through how it works, in the room and in the chat.

How the schedule works

The General Meeting lands on the 4th Monday of every month at about 6:00 PM, hybrid, so you can come in person or join from home. The next one is Monday, July 27, 2026, and it lands every 4th Monday after that.

Working groups set their own schedules. The Public Transportation group, for example, meets on its standing 2nd Monday, while others meet as announced. The Steering Committee meets the 2nd Sunday of each month to keep the chapter running; that one is internal rather than a public on-ramp.

About the hybrid format

Every General Meeting runs online via Zoom and at an in-person spot announced with the agenda. RSVP on Action Network and the meeting link comes to you, so you can join from your couch or in the room.

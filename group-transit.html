Working group

Public Transportation

Frequent, affordable, dignified KAT service for everyone who rides.

Join this group All working groups

What we do

Buses are how a lot of Knoxville gets to work, to the doctor, and to the grocery store. We organize riders and neighbors to win frequent, affordable, dignified KAT service for everyone who depends on it, not just a route map that looks fine on paper.

We are a standing team inside the chapter. We talk with riders at the stop, show up where transit decisions get made, and bring proposals back to the chapter when a fight needs the whole room behind it.

What we are working on

Our focus stays close to the ground:

  • Defending and expanding KAT bus service, so routes run often enough and late enough to actually use.
  • Rider advocacy at the city, turning out to public meetings and budget hearings where service and fares are decided.
  • Bus-stop and sidewalk infrastructure, because a route is only as good as the safe, sheltered, walkable way to reach it.

What you would actually do

You do not need any experience, and you do not need to know transit policy. Newcomers usually start with something small and concrete:

  • Ride a route with us and help note where it falls short.
  • Hand a short rider survey to a few people at a stop.
  • Come to a city meeting with the group and add one more body in the room.
  • Help draft a flyer, take photos, or just take notes so the rest of us can keep up.

Membership

Join Knoxville DSA

Dues start where you can, and waivers are available, no questions asked. When you join, you get a real vote in a democratic chapter and a community of neighbors who have your back.

Start your sign-up Come to a meeting first

Why join

Knoxville Area DSA is run by its members, not by a board far from East Tennessee. When you join, you can vote at the monthly General Meeting on what the chapter takes on, where its money goes, and who leads it. That vote is the whole point: this is a place where ordinary people decide together instead of waiting to be asked.

Membership is also the door into the work. Our nine working groups are organizing for frequent and affordable KAT bus service, tenant power and eviction defense, on-the-job organizing across town and on the UT campus, queer liberation, eco-socialism, and more. You can try one, try a few, or just come read with the Little Red Schoolhouse until you find your footing.

Most of all, you get people. Folks who knock doors with you, save you a seat, and remember your name the second time. We believe that both the economy and society should be run democratically to meet human needs, not to make profits for a few, and we would rather build that with you than talk about it without you.

3

Come to New Member 101

A friendly 60-minute intro to the chapter, held every month. We will make sure you know when the next one is.

Dues and waivers

National dues are a sliding scale, so you set what works for your budget. Waivers are available, no questions asked. Coming to meetings and working groups is always free, member or not.

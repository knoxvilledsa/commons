Community

Find help and lend a hand

The chapter organizes for a Knoxville that works for working people, and part of that is pointing neighbors toward help that already exists. This page gathers mutual aid, food access, and other community resources across Knoxville and East Tennessee.

The list

Resources by category

Grouped by what you need help with. Every listing links out to the organization itself; the chapter does not run these programs, only points toward them.

Know a resource that belongs here

This list grows through the community, not just chapter staff. If you know a mutual aid group, food bank, or other resource that should be listed, or if you are the one who offered to share a trans resource directory, reach the chapter through Contact.

Why we publish this

Mutual aid is solidarity, not charity: neighbors meeting neighbors’ needs directly, with no means test and no strings attached. An organization that works for a better world has a responsibility to help people find the resources that already exist to support them, whether or not those resources come from the chapter itself.

This page will keep growing as members and neighbors point us to more organizations doing that work in Knoxville and East Tennessee.

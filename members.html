How the chat works

Getting on the chapter chat

Ask to be added

The chapter chat is members-only, so it is not a link you can click from here. Send a note through Contact and a member of the Communications Committee will add you, usually within a day or two.

The chat runs on the chapter’s Community Agreements: treat each other with care, keep what is shared in the room, and assume good faith. Reading them before you post is the fastest way to feel at home. They are adopted chapter practice, summarized on the About page and posted in full in the documents library.

Grow the chapter

Bring someone with you

You are already in, so here is the highest-leverage thing you can do next: invite a friend, a neighbor, or a coworker. Point them at Start Here, and bring them to the next meeting.

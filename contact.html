Harassment or safety concerns go to the chapter’s elected Harassment Grievance Officer, who handles them under the Code of Conduct. You can use Forms A, B, or C in the chapter documents.

Harassment and safety concerns

A harassment or safety concern does not have to go through the general form above. It can go straight to the chapter’s elected Harassment Grievance Officer, who is trained to handle these reports confidentially under the Code of Conduct and the Harassment & Grievance Policy.

The grievance forms (Forms A, B, and C) and the full policy are published with the chapter’s documents. If you’re unsure where to start or you just need to talk it through first, that’s okay too. Reach out and someone will help you find the right next step.

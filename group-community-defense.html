Get Involved / Community Defense

Working group

Community Defense

Rapid response for planned actions and emerging community needs.

Join this group All working groups

What we do

When neighbors face a threat and there isn’t time to deliberate, Community Defense shows up. We organize rapid response for planned actions, court support, and moments when a community member needs people in their corner fast. The work ranges from coordinating turnout at a demonstration to making sure someone facing a hearing isn’t there alone.

We are a standing team inside the chapter. We coordinate through a rapid-response chat so we can move quickly when something comes up, and we bring the chapter in when a situation calls for a bigger room.

What we are working on

Our focus stays close to the ground:

  • Rapid-response turnout for planned direct actions, rallies, and demonstrations in Knoxville and East Tennessee.
  • Court and community support for neighbors facing hearings, immigration proceedings, or other moments when a visible presence matters.
  • Know-your-rights resources and preparation so people can show up informed, not just willing.

What you would actually do

You don’t need any prior organizing experience. Newcomers usually start with something immediate and low-stakes:

  • Join the rapid-response chat and respond when a call goes out for a specific action.
  • Show up to a demonstration, hearing, or action as one more body in the room.
  • Help with logistics: making signs, coordinating rides, or keeping a headcount.
  • Share know-your-rights information in your own networks before an action.
